Okay Ariel, listen up 👀 — you got money to invest, and we are NOT about to let it sit around doing nothing like a watermelon on a porch in July. Below are three safe, boring-on-purpose example portfolios. Boring is good when it comes to money. Punch in your numbers, hit the button, and let the chaos (and the compounding) begin.

🐒 The "Don't Touch It" Portfolio

Low-risk, sleep-easy money. Good for an emergency fund or money you want safe but still growing a little faster than a checking account.

60% High-Yield Savings (~4.5% APY) 40% Treasury Bonds / Bond Fund (~4% avg)

🛞 The "Cruisin' Comfortably" Portfolio

Medium-safe, a step up in growth. Mostly index funds — boring, diversified, historically solid. Good for a 5-15 year goal, like a house down payment or "I just want it to grow" money.

50% S&P 500 Index Fund 30% Bond Index Fund 20% CDs / Money Market

🚬🍾 The "Grown Folks Business" Portfolio

Longer time horizon, slightly more growth-tilted but still diversified and steady. For retirement-style money you won't touch for 20-30 years. Slow burn, big payoff — just like the cigar.

70% Total Stock Market Index Fund 20% International Index Fund 10% Bond Index Fund
